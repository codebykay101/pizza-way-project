Rewards

How do I earn Rewards?

Earn credit for each qualifying visit by signing into your account during online ordering or by entering your phone number on the payment device in-store. As your qualifying visits start to accumulate, you'll unlock purchase-based Rewards tailored to you and your favorite items. You can look forward to free items, birthday pizza, dollars off your order, and other exclusive Rewards.

Each day, up to two qualifying visits can count towards purchase-based Rewards. Qualifying visits must be three hours apart and include a minimum spend of $4 before taxes and discounts

How does Pizza Way's birthday Reward work?

If you’re a MySLICE Rewards® member and you’ve made a qualifying visit at least 180 days prior to your birthday, a Birthday Reward will be added to your account on your birthday. Redeem online or in-store within seven days for one regular price* pizza – up to $16 value. Not a Rewards member? Sign up now!

*Excludes Specials, FAVES, XLNY, $6.99 Medium, Tuesday & Friday pizza deals. Cannot be combined with other offers or coupons.

How do I veiw my Rewards?

Sign in to your account. Then click on the profile icon to view your MySLICE Rewards® and see how many visits to your next Reward.

How do I redeem my Rewards?

REDEEM ONLINE
Sign in to your account during online ordering and follow these three simple steps:
1. Select the items you want to purchase and add them to your cart.
2. Add an eligible Reward item to your cart.
3. Go to your cart and click + ADD next to the Reward you wish to redeem. Then click
Checkout to see your savings!

REDEEM IN-STORE Simply enter your phone number at the payment device. The cashier will notify you of any available Rewards and give you the option to redeem one at that time.

NOTE: Only one (1) Reward may be applied per transaction. Discount can be used on one regular price item only.

Why can't I redeem my Reward?

Rewards cannot be applied to items on Special, including Tuesday and Friday deals. Only one Reward can be used per order, and Rewards cannot be combined with promo codes.

To redeem a Reward online, add an eligible Reward item to your cart. Go to your cart and click + ADD next to the Reward you wish to redeem. Then click Checkout to see your savings.

Why can't I access my account?

If you've completed the steps to create an account and you're unable to sign in, you may
need to reset your password: Go to the Sign In page and click Forgot Password. Then, enter the email address used to create your account.

If that doesn't work, try to create a new account. Be sure to complete all the required fields and check the box to agree to Terms & Conditions. Then click Create Account.

If you're still having trouble, please contact one of our Brand Ambassadors for assistance by clicking the Chat button below.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the Sign In page and click Forgot Password. Then, enter the email address used to create your account.

If you can't remember which email address you used, please contact one of our Brand Ambassadors for assistance by clicking the Chat button below.

How do I update my account information?

To update your account information or change your password, Sign In to your MySLICE Rewards account. Then click on the profile icon and select Update Profile.
